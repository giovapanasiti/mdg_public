Strategy Before Technology

Define >> Implement >> Review >> Support

We usually spend lot of time, up front, getting to know who our clients are, who their customers are, how they like to talk to them, and most important what are their goals.

We want our clients to be part of our development process.

Our development process has 6 phases and in each and everyone of these we keep the client posted with regular updates and we continuosly listen for feedbacks to iterate the software while it is borning to make it a tailored product.

The Process

Strategy

STRATEGY BEFORE TECHNOLOGY. We agree. We start every project with lot of thinking about what is the best way to do something. The best language or the database that best fit our client's needs.

Wireframing

Before even start to coding we sketch some wireframes. This will help us and the client to "sync" on what should the interface look-like or how he'd like the flow to be.

Development

Here all the magic happens. We use milestones. Everytime a milestone is reached an "alpha" version is released and the client can test it so we start getting some feedback. Doing so we make the product grow up together with the people who will need to use it.

Optimization

Once the product is finished it usually needs to be optimized. We make the product ready for production. We also support clients who need continuous development so it's a back and forth between development and optimization.

Dev Ops

We also take cares of the deployment of the application, if requested, and optimize the deployment enviroment to make the application works smooth and flawless.

Support

Long time support is the key of success for many clients. Maybe you don't need continuous development but you need to make minor changes or something got broken and you need to fix it. Once we deliver an application or a software, our support is guaranteed.
