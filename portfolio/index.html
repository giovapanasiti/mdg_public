Personal Trainer’s Dashboard

A dashboard for a personal trainer and his/her students

I love to train and I usually do it with my personal trainer who has a studio right in the center of Rome, where I live in. He once told me his problem and I proposed him a project. This web application is what came out. He and his students are very satisfied.

It allows students to book an appointment which can be confirmed or not by the personal trainer in just one click.

The personal trainer can also assign diets, exercise andhave some cool dashboard to keep track of students’ progresses

  • Date: 2017-07-05
  • Client: Gionata D'Elia Personal Trainer
  • Category: Web Development

W3Security Managment

An custom CRM for a security agency in Rome

Our client has a huge security agency, with lots of operators, clients and suplliers. He needed a software to manage all these three kinds and also a way to assign a job to operator, keep track of a service they’ve sold, invoicing etc. We made it.

We also made a mobile application for the oeprators to check-in and check-out from the services they have been assigned to, keep track of services and schedules.
