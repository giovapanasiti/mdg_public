PERSONAL TRAINER MANAGER

A dashboard for a personal trainer and his/her students

I love to train and I usually do it with my personal trainer who has a studio right in the center of Rome, where I live in. He once told me his problem and I proposed him a project. This web application is what came out. He and his students are very satisfied.

It allows students to book an appointment which can be confirmed or not by the personal trainer in just one click.

The personal trainer can also assign diets, exercise andhave some cool dashboard to keep track of students’ progresses

  • Date: 2017-07-05
  • Client: Gionata D'Elia Personal Trainer
  • Category: Web Development

The Challenge

Growing and scaling a business brings more than one challenge. Our client came to a point where they needed a smarter solution to keep track of all their trainees’ workout schedules, goals, and diets.


Our Solution

Thanks to a combination of MongoDB, Meteor and React, we built an agile solution that brings everything in the same place for trainers and trainees. With their tablet, phone or computer, trainers can access a shared calendar to book and secure trainees’ workouts.
In the same platform, they have a personalised dashboard for each trainee, where they track achievements, diets, goals and progress. Nothing will be missed anymore.

SECURITY MANAGMENT

An custom CRM for a security agency in Rome

Our client has a huge security agency, with lots of operators, clients and suplliers. He needed a software to manage all these three kinds and also a way to assign a job to operator, keep track of a service they’ve sold, invoicing etc. We made it.

We also made a mobile application for the oeprators to check-in and check-out from the services they have been assigned to, keep track of services and schedules.

  • Date: 2017-09-09
  • Client: W3Group
  • Category: Web & Mobile Development

The Challenge

Our client came to us with a challenge. Being able to manage their whole workforce efficiently was the single most important part of their business, and they needed a smarter way of doing it.
They were dreaming of a world where they could track the operators’ positions, assign each operator to a location, or a client, and make everything ticks with their accounting system.


Our Solution

We’ve built a solid backend solution from the ground up using Ruby on Rails. On the frontend, we used React with Redux and Flutter to build two native mobile apps for iOS and Android.
Now, our client’s managers can use the web interface for assigning operators, generating invoices and send them to clients. The field operators have a modern and functional app that tracks their GPS position, shows them their schedule and shifts, and also notifies them if anything changes.
Finally, we built for our client a bespoke dashboard to interact with all the data they have about clients, invoices, operators and locations, so they can see at a glance what is going on, and be more productive.
